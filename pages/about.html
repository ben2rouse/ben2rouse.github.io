About Me

Hi, I’m Benjamin Rouse—a data analyst with a passion for turning raw data into meaningful insights. With a BBA in Computer Information Systems and hands-on experience in Power BI, SQL, Python, and Excel, I specialize in data visualization, reporting automation, and business intelligence.

Currently, I work as a Student Assessment Specialist at Southern Adventist University, where I develop Power BI reports that help analyze student success, graduate placement, departmental performance and much more.

Beyond my professional work, I have experience as a freelance web developer, building dynamic websites and managing databases with PHP, JavaScript, and SQL. I thrive on solving complex problems and leveraging data to drive smarter decisions.

When I’m not working with data, you’ll find me diving into business strategy, automation tools, and emerging technologies to refine my skills. I’m always eager to learn and take on new challenges that push my analytical and technical capabilities.
